A Pretty Good Frisbee Throw

Ryan Zuzelski

Hello. This page documents the process and results of a pretty good frisbee throw I made earlier today.
Introduction
Earlier today my friend Nate got off the phone with a mutual friend of ours, Karl, who was in town for a short time period and wanted to throw a frisbee around. Nate asked if I wanted to join them and I obliged. Karl arrived punctually and we grabbed a disc and walked down the street to Ravenna park. It was a beautiful day and I would have enjoyed the experience even if my frisbee throws were not very good, but over the course of the session there was one throw that stood out to me as pretty good.
